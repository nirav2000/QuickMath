    Fast Square Root Method

    1) Anchor Squares

    Memorize 1² to 30². Eg: 20²=400, 25²=625.

    2) Range Spotting

    Find two nearest perfect squares around the number.

    3) Last Digit Clue

    Perfect squares end in specific digits (0,1,4,5,6,9).

    4) Tight Estimate

    For non-perfect squares, estimate by closeness between anchors.
